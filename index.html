Frequently Asked Questions

What exactly is Fooocus AI?

Fooocus AI is an tool that helps you create best-looking images using AI. You can type a short description to create a new image or apply the Fooocus style to your photo to give it a clean, artistic look.

Is Fooocus AI free to use?

Yes, you can use Fooocus AI for free. We offer free access so you can try the image effect and create images without paying. Extra features may be added later, but a free option is always available.

How long does it take to create an image?

It usually takes only a few seconds. Sometimes it may take a little longer if many people are using it, but most images are ready very quickly.

What about my privacy? Are my images safe?

Yes, your images are safe. Your photos are used only to create your image effect and are not shared with anyone. They are stored for a short time and then removed.

Can I make changes or try different versions of the same image?

Yes, you can easily try again or change your text to get a new version. This helps you explore different looks without starting over.

Do I need to download anything to use Fooocus AI?

No download is needed. Fooocus AI works directly in your web browser. Just open the page, upload a photo or type a prompt, and start creating.
